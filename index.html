Virtual Mouse

A Virtual Mouse project is an innovative concept that aims to simulate and control mouse functionality without the need for a physical mouse device. It is especially useful in scenarios where a traditional mouse is unavailable, inaccessible, or not suitable for the task at hand. The virtual mouse can be controlled via various input methods such as touchpads, touchscreens, hand gestures, or even webcam-based gesture recognition.


My Third Project


Fetal Health Prediction using CTG

Fetal health prediction using CTG (Cardiotocography) is a critical area in obstetrics for monitoring the well-being of the fetus during pregnancy, particularly in high-risk pregnancies. CTG is a medical test that records the fetal heart rate (FHR) and uterine contractions, offering valuable insights into the fetus's health status. The goal of fetal health prediction is to analyze the CTG data to detect early signs of distress or abnormal conditions that may affect the fetus, and to predict potential complications that might require medical intervention.
